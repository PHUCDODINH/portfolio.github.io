-POP was qualified and upload on itch.io, the most prominent platform for idie game developer with an user base of over 100000 - the third highest only after Epic Games and Steam

-POP was a combination of designing, animating, and coding skills. In order to create POP, I had to take part in three game development courses on Skillshare and underwent numerous instruction videos on YouTube about game design and animation.

-For a beginner like me, POP really exceeded my expectations, as it gained over 200 downloads in its debut month.

-Beyond only having programming and design abilities, research is indispensable in the development of mobile games. The key in the development of Detroit is to conduct extensive research regarding player demand and the current most prominent genre in order to create a pleasure experience

-Detroit is completed only within a month. As a result of extensive research and commitment, the game was successfully accepted and published on GameJolt - one of the biggest game publishers.
